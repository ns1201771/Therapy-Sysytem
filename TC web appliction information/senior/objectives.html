Project objectives

Our project aims to achieve the following objectives:

• Using a commercially available stationary therapy cycle and modifying it into a workout machine.

• Designing the machine interface with a low cost and suitable size that helps the patient do their exercise at home and in their own time.

• Creating an adaptive 3D virtual environment that meets the needs of patient’s treatment.

• Communicating to the doctor, the results and logs of various exercise sessions as well as obtaining doctor’s input for changing the exercise settings.
